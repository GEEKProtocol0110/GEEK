Geek Protocol: Master Project Document

Version 1.5 | Sovereign Protocol Edition

1.0 Introduction & Vision

Geek Protocol is a decentralized, sovereign ecosystem for social learning and gaming, centered around a "Quiz2Earn" model. The protocol's mission is to build a platform that is owned and operated by its community of users and contributors, where geeks can learn, compete, and monetize their expertise.

2.0 Core Contributors & Governance

Geek Protocol is a decentralized initiative stewarded by its community and core contributors.

  • Initial Contributor (G): The protocol was architected and initiated by its founding developer, G.
  • Core Contributors: The protocol is actively sourcing other passionate builders through grants from the future community treasury.
  • The DAO: The ultimate goal is to transition all decision-making to the Geek Protocol DAO.

3.0 Core Gameplay & Gamification

3.1 The Geek Gauntlet

The primary single-player gameplay mode is a 100-question trivia challenge with increasing difficulty and time limits, designed for high engagement.

3.2 The Geek Arena (Multiplayer Suite)

Scheduled for post-launch development, this suite of real-time multiplayer modes is designed to foster community and viral growth.

  • The Duel (1v1 PVP): A head-to-head battle with optional `$GEEK` wagers.
  • Team Battle (2v2 PVP): A competitive mode requiring teamwork and strategy.
  • Party Mode (Social Co-op): A casual, cooperative mode for groups of friends to play together.
  • The Trivia Royale (Live Event): A massive, last-player-standing tournament hosted by A.C.E. with a significant prize pool.

3.3 Gamification & Learning Systems

A deep "meta-game" designed for long-term retention, including a "Geek's Journey" XP/level system, NFT achievements, collectible sets, a gamified daily login vault, and incentivized "Learn & Earn" modules.

4.0 The In-Game Economy

A player-owned economy facilitated through an in-game store for cosmetic and consumable items, and a peer-to-peer marketplace for trading NFT assets, all powered by `$GEEK`.

5.0 $GEEK Tokenomics

A self-sustaining economic model with a fixed total supply of 144 billion tokens and a 70/30 Recycle & Burn mechanism on all platform fees to ensure long-term deflationary pressure and a healthy rewards pool.

6.0 Content & Community Strategy

A decentralized content engine will allow users to submit, peer-review, and earn passive income from their own quiz questions, ensuring the content library is vast and constantly growing.

7.0 Brand & Character Guide

The protocol's identity is led by its two characters: **A.C.E.** (the sharp, analytical AI host) and **Giga** (the friendly, community-facing robot mascot).

8.0 Technical Architecture & Stack

The protocol will be built on a modern, scalable stack including React.js, Node.js, MongoDB, Redis, and IPFS with Pinata. All on-chain logic will be handled by KRC-20 smart contracts on the Kaspa network.

9.0 Long-Term Vision (5+ Years)

The roadmap includes a multi-year vision to expand beyond entertainment into B2B verticals, including **"Geek Protocol for Education"** for schools, **"Geek Protocol: Pro"** for professional development, and a community-governed **Launchpad** to incubate new projects.
