1.0 The Vision: What is Geek Protocol?

In the digital universe, knowledge is power. Geek Protocol is a decentralized, sovereign ecosystem built on the high-speed Kaspa network. Our mission is to build a platform owned and operated by its community, where geeks can learn, compete, and monetize their expertise.

2.0 The Ecosystem

  • The Geek Gauntlet & Challenges: The high-stakes trivia core of the platform, supplemented with daily, weekly, and monthly challenges to reward consistent engagement.
  • Top-Tier Gamification: A deep "meta-game" with XP, levels, NFT achievements, and tradable collectible sets to drive long-term retention.
  • Community Content Engine: The protocol is powered by its users, who can submit, peer-review, and earn passive income from their own high-quality quiz questions.

3.0 The $GEEK Token

$GEEK is the KRC-20 utility token that powers the entire ecosystem. Its purpose is to facilitate governance and all economic activity within the protocol. A 70/30 Recycle & Burn mechanism on all platform fees ensures a sustainable economy.

4.0 Core Contributors & Governance

Geek Protocol is a decentralized initiative stewarded by its community and core contributors.

  • Initial Contributor (G): The protocol was architected and initiated by its founding developer, G, whose personal story provides the "why" for the entire project.
  • Core Contributors: The protocol is actively sourcing other passionate builders through grants from the community treasury.
  • The DAO: The ultimate goal is to transition all decision-making to the Geek Protocol DAO, where `$GEEK` holders will govern the future of the ecosystem.
