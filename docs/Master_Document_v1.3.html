Geek Protocol: Master Project Document

Version 1.3 | Last Updated: July 7, 2025

1.0 Introduction & Vision

Geek Protocol is a Web3-based ecosystem for social learning and gaming, centered around a "Quiz2Earn" model. Our mission is to build the definitive decentralized platform where geeks can learn, compete, and monetize their expertise in a self-sustaining, community-driven economy.

1.1 Strategic Positioning

Based on competitive analysis, Geek Protocol will be positioned as the clear evolution of the Quiz-to-Earn model. Our strategy is to be more accessible than hardcore tournament platforms and more engaging and community-focused than generic trivia apps. Key strategic pillars include:

  • Character-Driven Branding: Leaning heavily into the unique personalities of A.C.E. and Giga to create a memorable and emotionally resonant brand.
  • Accessibility as a Weapon: Marketing will emphasize our "Play Anytime, Earn Anytime. No Buy-In Required" model to attract the widest possible audience.
  • Superior Gamification: The "Collectible Sets" and "Geek's Journey" systems will be core marketing points, highlighting our focus on long-term retention.
  • Community-First Ethos: The Community Content Engine will be promoted from day one to attract users who believe in building a platform, not just using one.

2.0 User Experience & Atmosphere

The platform is designed to evoke two distinct, powerful feelings:

  • The Social Hub ("The Discord Feel"): A bustling community headquarters achieved through live event chats, integrated Discord roles, and rich user profiles.
  • The Gameplay Drama ("The Millionaire Feel"): A focused, high-stakes "hot seat" experience during the quiz, achieved through UI/sound design and dramatic hosting by A.C.E.

3.0 Core Gameplay & Gamification

3.1 The Geek Gauntlet

  • Format: 10 rounds of 10 questions each.
  • Progression: Increasing difficulty across Apprentice, Adept, and Master tiers.
  • Pacing: A strict 15-second timer per question with a time bonus.
  • Lifelines: "50/50," "Ask the Fandom," and "Extra Time."

3.2 Future Game Modes

  • Monthly Grand Gauntlet (Year 1): A live, scheduled, A.C.E.-hosted tournament with a massive prize pool and a `$GEEK` buy-in.
  • Trivia Royale (Year 2): A high-energy, 100-player elimination mode where the slowest to answer correctly are eliminated each round.

3.3 The "Geek's Journey" Gamification System

  • Progression Map & XP: A visual map where users level up and unlock rewards.
  • Achievement System: Tiered achievements that grant XP and non-tradable NFT badges.
  • Collectible Sets: "Sticker album" mechanic to drive collection and trading.
  • The Vault: A gamified daily login bonus.

3.4 Incentivized Learning & Participation

  • Learn & Earn Modules: Pre-quiz tutorials that grant rewards and multipliers.
  • Exploration Bonus: A bonus for trying new categories.
  • Daily, Weekly & Monthly Challenges: A system of rotating goals and rewards.
